About DSR

District Survey Report for Sustainable Sand Mining

A District Survey Report is the official district-level document used to assess mineral availability, river morphology, replenishment, environmental safeguards, transport routes, and compliance requirements before sand mining activities are reviewed.

The DSR workflow covers district profile, river systems, mining lease context, cross-section studies, mineral estimation, replenishment assessment, transport routes, environmental safeguards, authority review, e-sign approval, and final report generation.

This portal keeps the complete process in one place so district teams can upload chapter PDFs, maintain annexures, preview the live report, track progress, and submit a compliant final DSR under the EMGSM 2020 framework.
